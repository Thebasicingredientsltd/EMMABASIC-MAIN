## Section 1 — What is clean label? (dark / ink background)

**Heading:** What is clean label?

**Paragraph 1:**
Clean label — or 'Clean Dec' as food technologists call it — simply means products made without additives. The Basic Ingredients Ltd is a BRCGS-certified Grade AA food company built on this principle.

**Paragraph 2:**
At Emma Basic, we go further than just removing additives. When developing a product we eliminate UPFs like refined oils, reduce plastic in packaging, and allow only a minimal number of ingredients that are nourishing and natural — to support real well-being.
